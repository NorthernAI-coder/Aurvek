## Short answer

You can ask the AI to generate short videos with audio during a conversation. Describe the video you want, and the AI will create an 8-second clip using Google VEO. The video appears directly in the chat when finished.

## Steps

1. Open a conversation in the chat.
2. Type a message describing the video you want. For example: "Generate a video of waves crashing on a beach at sunset."
3. To specify an aspect ratio, include it in your prompt (e.g., `16:9`, `9:16`, `1:1`). The default is 16:9.
4. To exclude unwanted elements, use natural phrases like "without text" or "avoid close-ups" in your description.
5. Send the message. A progress indicator will appear, updating you as the video is generated.
6. Video generation can take up to 6 minutes. The chat will show periodic progress updates.
7. The finished video appears in the conversation once ready.

## Notes

- Video generation costs significantly more than image generation. Make sure you have sufficient account balance.
- The AI generates 8-second video clips with audio.
- Generation can take several minutes. Progress messages will update in the chat while you wait.
- If generation exceeds 6 minutes, a timeout error will appear. You can try again with a simpler prompt.
- The generated video is saved as an MP4 file in the conversation and can be replayed later.

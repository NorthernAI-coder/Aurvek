## Short answer

Web search lets the AI access up-to-date information from the internet while answering your questions. It is enabled by default and can be toggled on or off from the plus (+) menu in the chat. You do not need to do anything special to trigger a search -- just ask a question that requires current information and the AI will search automatically.

## Steps

1. Open a conversation in the chat.
2. Click the **+** button at the bottom-left of the message input area.
3. Look for the **Web search** item with a toggle switch.
4. Click the toggle to turn web search on or off. Your preference is saved to your account and persists across sessions.
5. Type your message as usual. When web search is active, the AI will search the web automatically if it determines that current information is needed.

## Notes

- Some prompts may force web search to always be on or always be off. When web search is forced on, the toggle appears active but cannot be changed. When the prompt disables web search, the toggle is hidden entirely.
- The web search toggle only appears when a conversation is selected.
- You can choose between different search engines (Native or Perplexity) in your account settings. See the **web_search_modes** article for details.
- When the AI performs a web search, it may include source citations in its response.
- The plus (+) button shows a small indicator dot when web search or other features are active.

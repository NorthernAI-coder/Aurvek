## Short answer

To link your Telegram account, open the Aurvek bot in Telegram and share your phone number when prompted. The bot matches your phone number against your Aurvek account and links them automatically.

## Steps

1. Open Telegram and search for the Aurvek bot (ask your administrator for the bot username if you do not know it).
2. Tap **Start** or send any message to the bot.
3. The bot will ask you to share your phone number. Tap the **"Share my phone number"** button that appears.
4. Telegram will ask you to confirm. Accept the request to share your contact.
5. The bot looks up the phone number in Aurvek. If it matches your account, you will see a welcome message confirming the link.
6. You can now send text messages, voice messages, or photos directly to the bot and receive AI responses.

## Notes

- Your Aurvek account must have the same phone number that is registered on your Telegram account. If they do not match, the bot will not be able to link them.
- You must share your own phone number, not someone else's contact. The bot verifies that the contact belongs to the sender.
- Each Telegram account can only be linked to one Aurvek account. If the Telegram account is already linked to a different user, you will see an error message.
- If your Aurvek account is disabled, the bot will not allow linking.
- If phone verification is required by the platform administrator, you must verify your phone number in your Aurvek account settings before using the Telegram bot.
- Once linked, the bot automatically creates a conversation for you. You can view this conversation from the Aurvek web interface as well.
- A conversation cannot be on both Telegram and WhatsApp simultaneously. Assigning it to Telegram removes it from WhatsApp, and vice versa.
- Type `!help` after linking to see all available commands.

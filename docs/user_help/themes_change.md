## Short answer

Aurvek has 13 visual themes you can switch between at any time from the navigation bar. They range from clean and minimal to richly animated, and one of them (Neko Glass) lets you customize nearly every visual detail. Your choice is saved in your browser and applies instantly across all pages.

## Steps

1. Click your **profile menu** in the top-right navigation bar.
2. Hover over (or tap on mobile) the **Theme** submenu item.
3. Select a theme from the list. The page reloads with the new look.

## Available themes

### Everyday themes

- **Default** -- A comfortable dark theme with soft grays and blue accents. Familiar if you have used Discord or similar apps. A solid all-rounder.
- **Light** -- A clean, bright theme for people who prefer light backgrounds. Easy on the eyes during daytime use.
- **Writer** -- Warm sepia and cream tones, like writing on aged paper. Designed for long reading sessions. Text uses serif fonts for a literary feel.
- **E-Ink** -- Pure black and white with zero decoration. Mimics the look of an e-reader. Everything is stripped to essentials: no colors, no rounded corners, no distractions.

### Personality themes

- **Coder** -- Inspired by VS Code's Dark+ color scheme. Monospace fonts in menus, blinking cursor effects, and a coding-editor atmosphere throughout.
- **Terminal** -- Green text on black, just like a classic computer terminal. Blinking cursors on titles, a scanline sweep effect, and a soft green glow on everything. Feels like hacking into a mainframe.
- **Frutiger Aero** -- A nostalgic trip to the glossy, translucent style of Windows Vista and early 2000s interfaces. Soft blue gradients, shiny buttons, and glass-like panels everywhere.
- **Memphis** -- Bold, loud, and unapologetic. Inspired by the 1980s Memphis design movement: clashing colors (yellow, pink, teal), hard geometric shadows, and elements that tilt and bounce on hover.
- **Katari Shoji** -- An elegant, refined look with gold and navy tones. Uses premium serif and sans-serif fonts for a luxury feel, with subtle golden shimmer effects on hover.

### Seasonal themes

- **Christmas** -- Snow falls gently across your screen while you chat. Festive red and green colors with decorative touches on messages and buttons. Best enjoyed in December.
- **Halloween** -- A spooky atmosphere with a horror-movie font, floating messages, and bat silhouettes in the background. Messages come with ghost and pumpkin decorations.
- **Valentine's Day** -- Soft pinks and a romantic handwriting-style font. Gentle heartbeat animations and rounded, cozy shapes everywhere.

### Fully customizable

- **Neko Glass** -- An anime-inspired theme where the entire interface becomes translucent glass floating over a wallpaper. What makes it unique: a dedicated settings panel lets you upload your own wallpaper, adjust how transparent and blurry the glass is, pick the color of your chat bubbles, change the accent color, and much more. See the dedicated article for full details.

## Notes

The 13 themes: **Default** (comfortable dark, Discord-like), **Light** (clean bright), **Writer** (warm sepia tones, serif fonts, paper feel), **E-Ink** (pure black and white, e-reader style), **Coder** (VS Code Dark+ feel, monospace fonts), **Terminal** (green on black, blinking cursors, hacker glow), **Frutiger Aero** (glossy 2000s Windows Vista style), **Memphis** (bold 80s colors, bouncy animations), **Katari Shoji** (elegant gold and navy, luxury fonts), **Christmas** (falling snow, festive decorations), **Halloween** (horror font, floating messages, bat backgrounds), **Valentine's Day** (romantic pink, handwriting font, heartbeat animations), **Neko Glass** (fully customizable: upload your own wallpaper, adjust glass transparency, pick bubble colors and more).

- Your theme preference is stored in your browser. If you log in from a different browser or device, you will start with the Default theme.
- If a team manager has locked a specific theme for your account, the theme selector will be hidden and you will not be able to change it.
- Some themes include animated effects (falling snow, floating messages, glowing text). These are lightweight and should not affect performance on modern devices.

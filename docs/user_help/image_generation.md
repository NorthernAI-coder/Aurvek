## Short answer

You can ask the AI to generate images during a conversation. Simply describe the image you want in your message, and the AI will recognize your intent and create it. The generated image appears directly in the chat. You can optionally specify an aspect ratio in your prompt.

## Steps

1. Open a conversation in the chat.
2. Type a message describing the image you want. For example: "Generate an image of a sunset over the ocean" or "Create a portrait of a cat wearing a hat."
3. To specify an aspect ratio, include it in your prompt using the format `width:height`. Supported ratios: `1:1`, `16:9`, `9:16`, `4:3`, `3:4`. For example: "Draw a landscape of mountains 16:9."
4. Send the message. A "Generating image..." status will appear while the image is being created.
5. The generated image appears in the chat once ready.

## Notes

- Image generation requires sufficient account balance. If your balance is too low, you will see an error message.
- The AI decides when to generate an image based on your message content. You do not need to use a specific command -- natural language requests work.
- The default aspect ratio is 1:1 (square) if none is specified.
- Generation may take a few seconds depending on the engine. If it takes too long, a timeout message will appear.
- The generated image is saved in the conversation and can be viewed later.

## Short answer

Neko Glass is the most customizable theme on the platform. Beyond a visual style, it turns the entire interface into translucent glass panels floating over a wallpaper that you can change. A dedicated settings panel lets you fine-tune virtually everything: the wallpaper, how transparent and blurry the glass looks, the color of your chat bubbles, text colors, layout width, and more.

## Steps

1. Switch to the **Neko Glass** theme from the profile menu (top-right > Theme > Neko Glass).
2. A small **floating button** (palette icon) appears on the right side of the screen. Click it to open the **Glass Settings** panel.
3. The panel has five sections you can explore. All changes apply instantly:
   - **Layout**: column width, font size, bubble roundness, page text color.
   - **Glass Effect**: upload your own wallpaper (up to 5 MB), choose a glass tint (White, Charcoal, Midnight, Ocean, Amethyst, Rose, or custom), adjust blur intensity, glass opacity, and wallpaper dimming.
   - **User Messages** (chat): bubble color (Indigo, Purple, Rose, Teal, Emerald, Amber, or custom), bubble opacity, and text color.
   - **Bot Messages** (chat): bubble color (White Frost, Dark Glass, Smoke, Blush, Lavender, Mint, or custom), bubble opacity, and text color.
   - **UI Accent**: interface color for buttons and links (Indigo, Purple, Teal, Pink, or Green).
4. To start over, use the **Reset All** button at the bottom of the panel. It restores defaults and removes any custom wallpaper.

## The Glass Settings panel

The panel has five collapsible sections:

### Layout

- **Column Width** -- Choose how wide the content area is: Narrow, Medium, Wide, Wider, or Full screen.
- **Font Size** (chat only) -- Adjust the text size in chat messages.
- **Bubble Roundness** (chat only) -- Choose from Sharp corners to fully rounded bubbles.
- **Page Text** (non-chat pages) -- Switch between light text, dark text, or pick a custom color.

### Glass Effect

This is the core of the theme:

- **Wallpaper** -- Upload your own image (up to 5 MB) or use the default one. Your wallpaper is stored privately in your account, so it follows you across devices when you are logged in.
- **Glass Tint** -- Pick the base color of the glass panels. Six presets (White, Charcoal, Midnight, Ocean, Amethyst, Rose) or choose any color you want.
- **Blur Intensity** -- Control how frosted the glass looks, from completely clear to heavily blurred.
- **Glass Opacity** -- How solid or transparent the panels are. Lower values let more of the wallpaper show through.
- **Wallpaper Dim** -- Darken the wallpaper so text stays readable. Useful for bright wallpapers.

### User Messages (chat only)

- **Bubble Color** -- Choose the color of your own message bubbles. Six presets (Indigo, Purple, Rose, Teal, Emerald, Amber) plus a custom color picker.
- **Bubble Opacity** -- How transparent your message bubbles are.
- **Text Color** -- White, Cream, Dark, or a custom color for your message text.

### Bot Messages (chat only)

Same controls as User Messages, with presets designed for readability: White Frost, Dark Glass, Smoke, Blush, Lavender, and Mint.

### UI Accent

- **Interface Color** -- Changes the color of buttons, links, and interactive elements across the interface. Five options: Indigo, Purple, Teal, Pink, or Green.

## Resetting everything

At the bottom of the Glass Settings panel, the **Reset All** button restores every setting to its default and removes any custom wallpaper. This brings the theme back to its original look.

## Notes

- Your customization settings are stored in your browser. If you switch browsers or clear data, they reset to defaults. Your custom wallpaper is stored on the server, so it persists across devices.
- The Glass Settings button only appears while Neko Glass is active. Switching to another theme hides it.
- On the chat page you get extra controls (bubble colors, font size, roundness) not shown on other pages.
